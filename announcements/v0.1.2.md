# reinhardt-web v0.1.2

## Highlights

This is a small maintenance release that corrects a versioning oversight introduced during the v0.1.1 stable release cycle. The `reinhardt-testkit-macros` crate was accidentally omitted from the `reinhardt` version group in the release-plz configuration, meaning it was not bumped in lockstep with the rest of the reinhardt family during that release.

Going forward, `reinhardt-testkit-macros` is now included in the coordinated version group, ensuring that all crates in the reinhardt ecosystem are versioned and released together. Users who depend on `reinhardt-testkit-macros` can expect consistent version alignment with `reinhardt-web` and related crates in future releases.
